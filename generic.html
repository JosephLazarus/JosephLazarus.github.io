Addressing Missing Values Data Scientists rarely work on perfect data and thus a large percentage of effort is devoted to steps 2 and 3. In our analysis of the beer data set we found 42% of the beers are missing IBU information. 2.6% are mising ABV information. We evaluated 3 methods to impute missing values.
